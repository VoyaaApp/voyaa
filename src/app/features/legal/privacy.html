Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Voyaa, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, date of birth, nationality
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio
  • Content: Photos, videos, and text you post
  • Usage Data: Interactions such as likes, comments, follows, bookmarks, and messages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Display your content to other users
  • Enable social features (following, messaging, commenting)
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse
  • Improve the App experience

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore) with servers located in Europe (London, europe-west2). Media files are stored on Cloudinary's content delivery network.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your content is visible to other authenticated users of the App. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (authentication, database), Cloudinary (media hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data through your profile
  • Correction: Update your information via profile editing
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data through Settings
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data

6. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from the Settings page. Upon deletion, we will remove your profile, posts, videos, messages, and all associated data. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

7. Children's Privacy

Voyaa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We implement security measures including Firebase Authentication and Firestore Security Rules to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

9. Cookies

Voyaa uses Firebase Authentication which stores session tokens locally on your device. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the App.
